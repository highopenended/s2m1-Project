Overview

Ryan George is a Canadian YouTube influencer who is best known for his videos wherein he plays two characters: a producer and the person pitching an idea for a movie or show.

He started out with homemade videos, then moved on to help provide content for popular YouTube channel, Screen Rant. Eventually, he branched out on his own and now has two primary channels: Ryan George and Pitch Meetings.

Screen Rant

Ryan George launched his first Pitch Meeting episodes on Screen Rant in 2017 to mixed reviews, but quickly found his rhythm and gained popularity with viewers. Many viewers would ignore the bulk of the channel's content, only visiting with a new Pitch Meeting video had been released.
